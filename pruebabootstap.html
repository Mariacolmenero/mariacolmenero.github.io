Born in Granada , two months ago I left the architectural firm where i worked an Madrid to complete my training as a designer. Currently I work as an assistant Erretes and Trazos_Pro and community manager of the Master of Communication and Strategic Design , collated with the same care as a student . In 2009 I joined the association of young architects from Granada " Aseja " and since then I have taken part in the image of delegation of students from the ETSAG , in the image of the demonstrations " 360 6 +0 " against reforms education of Bologna in organizing various events and in 2011 I was art director of the magazine " Lookark " the ETSAG . Motivated by the desire to know other cities , I moved to Italy to complete my studies in architecture and learn another language, using the experience to travel and see much of Europe . From my early days as a student at ETSAG , I've been obsessed with work and I have combined my academic training with real work . I have worked with several architectural firms , I have enjoyed the collaboration grants subaddress ETSAG and have worked for the public administration in the planning department . I am passionate about creativity , I believe in attention to detail in the design and its power to connect with people. Specialties : retail , home staging , strategy, design , publications, etc. .
